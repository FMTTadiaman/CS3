Authors Pov

The Author's POV novel is a popular light novel covering Action, Romance, and Fantasy genres. Written by the Author Entrail_JI. It is a story about a an author that believes he reincarnated on his own novel(Descent of the Hero), but later discovered that the world he "lived in" was just an imagination cause by his past self to create a scenario were he helps kevin(the main character of the novel) grow and kill the demon king using his knowledge about his "novel".
