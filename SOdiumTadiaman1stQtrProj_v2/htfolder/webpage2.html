Why poverty is a problem

problem 1: What causes food shortages? Poverty is the leading cause of hunger worldwide, both in rich and low- and middle-income countries. Most hungry people live in extreme poverty.

Problem 2: lack of shelter. poverty is one of the reasons why people are unable to have a home, because of poverty people are unable to have a stable income to save and buy a house.

Problem 3: lack of income. the reason why peopel are unable to access food, shelter, clothing and healthcare, is because of the lack of income from their jobs. the World Bank has defined extreme poverty as people living on less than $1.90 a day, measured using the international poverty line.
