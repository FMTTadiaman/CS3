What are the effects of Poverty?

Hunger

Hunger, a global problem cause by the lack of food and a sudden increase in the population or overpopulation. Hunger is also one of the effects of poverty, it is a situation where an individual is unable to buy food due to its price.

Homelessness

Another effects of poverty. Homelessness, a situation in which an indiviual or a group of people lives in the street, because of the inability to acquire a permanent place to live in.

Lack of education

Because of the lack of money, parents are force to make their kids drop out, causing them to lack the education they need to get into good paying jobs, which leads to working in harsh conditions to barely live.
