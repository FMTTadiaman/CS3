Why poverty is a problem

Health

Health: People living in poverty are more likely to suffer from poor health outcomes due to lack of access to healthcare, nutritious food, and safe living conditions. This can lead to chronic diseases and reduced life expectancy.

Education

Education: Children living in poverty are less likely to have access to quality education, which can limit their opportunities and future success.

Economic

Economic instability: Poverty can lead to instability in individuals' and families' lives, making it harder for them to plan for the future and make ends meet.
